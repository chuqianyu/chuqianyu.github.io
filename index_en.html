About Me

Hi!

I'm Qianyu Chu and have graduated from Photonic Systems Laboratory (PSL), School of Precision Instrument and Optoelectronic Engineering, Tianjin University, Tianjin, China. During the master's degree, My supervisors are Professor Guifang Li and Professor Lin Zhang.

My research interests encompass a diverse range of topics, including Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), Image Signal Processing (ISP), AI-ISP, High Performance Computing, Optical Computing, AI for Science, and Reinforcement Learning. I have published more than 10 papers.

In addition, I have a strong interest in the harmonica. All the time, I play the harmonica unremittingly. I have participated in the Panhu Harmonica Music Festival as the contestant. That time left me with profound and wonderful memories.
