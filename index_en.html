Project Experience

Research on Weighting Strategy Based on Sample Difficulty in Deep Learning
Sep 2021 - Oct 2022
Project Role: Research on Empowerment Strategies in Deep Learning Tasks
-- In the DL sample weighted priority mode, in addition to the easy priority and difficult priority modes, it is proved that medium difficulty priority and both-ends priority enriches exist the sample weighting strategy
-- Propose a difficulty-weighted general optimization objective function based on KL divergence and a flexible data-driven weighting function FlexW function, so as to achieve four priority modes through the adjustment of hyperparameters; the FlexW function has achieved good results in clean sample data sets, noise sample data sets and unbalanced sample datasets.
-- This method is applied to the ResNet34 model on the image classification problem of CIFAR10 and CIFAR100 data sets, which is better than Focal Loss and SPL classification The accuracy rate increased by about 5%; this method was applied to the YOLOv4 model for target detection on the PASCAL VOC dataset, and the accuracy rate increased by about 2%
